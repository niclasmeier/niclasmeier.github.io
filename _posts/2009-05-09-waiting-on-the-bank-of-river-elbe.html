This weekend (8.-10. May) is Hafengeburtstag</a> in my home town of Hamburg. This fair is celebrated every year. This year the Patrouille Suisse</a> air acrobatic team from the swiss air force show their skills. This weekend (8.-10. May) is Hafengeburtstag</a> in my home town of Hamburg. This fair is celebrated every year. This year the Patrouille Suisse</a> air acrobatic team from the swiss air force show their skills.

After I heard the F-5 fighter jets yesterday twice in the city I decided to got to the banks of the Elbe to see, if I can fetch a look. It was a sunny day, so I was in no hurry. But after 45 minutes waiting I decided to leave, without spotting the planes. On the news</a> I discovered that the plan was stripped-down due to some complaints. Maybe tomorrow I'll have some luck ;-)

But the time was not wasted at all. I took some pictures of boats</a> sailing to the St. Pauli Landungsbrücken</a>. Some guys deployed their mobile DJ station also, so I enjoyed some chilly electro beats too.
